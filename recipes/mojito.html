Mojito Recipe

Ingredients

  • 40 ml Light Rum
  • 30 ml Lime Juice
  • 30 ml Simple Syrup
  • 40 ml Club Soda
  • Mint

Instructions

  1. Pour lime juice and simple syrup into a glass.
  2. Add a bunch of mint leaves.
  3. Muddle the mint leaves together with the lime and simple syrup.
  4. Add rum and crushed ice.
  5. Garnish with a straw, a srig of mint.
  6. Enjoy your mojito!
