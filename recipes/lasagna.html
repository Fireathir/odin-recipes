Lasagna

Cheap Lasagna

What is this lasagna?

Lasagna is a smart food. Make a large amount in a big pan and you'll have a lot of portions to put into the freezer. Here's a vegetarian option. Make a double size to have more to put in the freezer afterwards.

This lasagna is both good for vegetarians and people who can't handle dairy.

Ingredients for the mixture

  • 1 Onion
  • 2 Dl red lentils, dried
  • 4 Dl smashed tomatoes
  • 3 Dl water
  • 2 Carrots
  • 1 cube of vegetable broth
  • 1 msk of tomatopure
  • Two garlic pieces
  • 1 msk herbs of desired variety
  • Salt and pepper
  • Oil to fry in

Ingredients for the cheese sauce

  • 1.d dl créme fraiche
  • 1 dl cheese, grated
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

Put the oven on 225 degrees celsius

Instructions for the filling

  1. Chop the onions and fry them until it's golden brown
  2. Wash the lentils in cold water. Put the lentils in the frying pan
  3. Put the smashed tomatoes in the pan.
  4. Grate the carrots and put them in the pan.
  5. Add the broth cubes and tomato pure.
  6. Let the mixture boil for about 10 minutes on medium heat. Stir once in a while.

Instructions for the cheese sauce

Don't let the cheese sauce boil

  1. Put the créme fraiche in a pan.
  2. Add the cheese while stiring it slowly.

Baking

  1. Butter a baking pan.
  2. Put a layer of tomato sauce in the bottom.
  3. Put lasagna plates on top
  4. Repeat step 1 through 3 until you've reached the top of the baking pan.
  5. Top of with a handful of cheese.
  6. Bake for 20 minutes in the oven.
  7. Enjoy!
